About Us

At Fake Review Detection, we are committed to enhancing online trust and transparency by using machine learning techniques to detect fake product reviews. Our platform analyzes English, Roman Nepali and Devnagari text using different algortihms like SVM, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, aswell as pre-trained architectures such as NepaliBERT. We employ a combination of TF-IDF, Word2Vec, and FastText embeddings, ensuring that our detection system is both robust and accurate. For Nepali reviews, we process both Devanagari and Romanized scripts, carefully preserving linguistic patterns including local slangs to capture subtle patterns in deceptive content. Our goal is simple: to empower consumers with reliable information by filtering out fake reviews, allowing businesses to maintain their credibility and users to make informed decisions.
